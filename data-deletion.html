Account & Data Deletion - Pulse60

At Nexus Labs, we respect your right to privacy. If you wish to delete your Pulse60 account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to our support team at nexuslabs.play@gmail.com.

Step 2: Use the subject line: "Pulse60 Account Deletion Request".

Step 3: In the body of the email, include the Google Email address that is currently linked to your Pulse60 account so we can locate your data.

Alternatively, you can delete your account directly from within the Pulse60 app by navigating to Settings > Delete Account (if available).

What Happens When You Request Deletion?

Once we receive your verified request, we will process it within 7-14 business days. The following actions will be taken:

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Your user profile information (Name, Email Address).
  • Your Google Authentication ID.
  • Your saved reading preferences, customized feed settings, and reading history.

Data That Will Be Kept (Retention Period):

  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-personally identifiable usage data may be retained indefinitely for the purpose of improving our AI models and service performance. This data cannot be traced back to you.
  • Legal Compliance: We may retain records of the deletion request itself for up to 1 year for legal and compliance auditing purposes.

If you have any questions, please contact us at nexuslabs.play@gmail.com.
